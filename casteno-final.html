Case Study 03 · B2B SaaS
Endpoint Security Platform
From single-channel dependency to a multi-channel acquisition system
$100+ → $30
CPL reduction
4 Channels
Meta, Google, LI, Reddit
HubSpot
CRM & pipeline tracking

The business was running LinkedIn-only lead generation with CPMs so high that most clicks bounced immediately. CPL was above $100 and volume was inconsistent.

Restructured the entire acquisition approach around cold, warm, and hot traffic logic — assigning each channel a defined role rather than competing for the same conversion. Meta and Google handled direct acquisition, LinkedIn and Reddit expanded the top of funnel feeding into retargeting sequences. Rebuilt remarketing and Google Ads structure, connected performance tracking through HubSpot to monitor lead quality beyond just form fills.

Channels: Meta, Google, LinkedIn, Reddit  |  Tools: HubSpot  |  Market: Europe  |  Scope: Multi-channel funnel, paid acquisition, remarketing, attribution
